About This App

This canvas allows you to search for any official Pokémon to view its data, or to use the fields as a template to design your own Fakemon

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl/Cmd + /: Focus on the search bar
  • Ctrl/Cmd + S: Toggle shiny Pokémon portrait
  • Ctrl/Cmd + B: Reset base Pokémon stats
  • Ctrl/Cmd + P: Reset Pokémon portrait
  • Ctrl/Cmd + L: Toggle theme
